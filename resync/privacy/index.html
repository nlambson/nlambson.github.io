Resync

A jet-lag and sleep-shift planner.

Privacy

Resync computes your adjustment plan on your device from the trip details you enter. It does not use your location.

  • Trips: the flights and times you enter are stored on your device. Time-zone math uses a built-in airport catalog — no location access is requested.
  • Notifications: light/sleep cues are scheduled locally on your device (and on your Apple Watch if paired).

Data collection

We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data. There is no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. Purchases are processed by Apple; subscription status is managed via RevenueCat (which receives an anonymous app user ID and the App Store transaction, never your name, contacts, or content).

Children

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.
