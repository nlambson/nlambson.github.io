Privacy Policy

OnThree runs on your device. Photos are captured and processed locally and are never uploaded to our servers.

  • Camera: used to capture your guided pose photos.
  • Photo library: used only to save photos you capture and to import an existing photo when you choose to.
  • Motion: used to level and frame the shot while you compose it. Motion data stays on your device.
  • Your photos: stored in your own photo library and on your device; they are never sent to us.

Purchases

We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data. There is no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. Purchases are processed by Apple; subscription status is managed via RevenueCat, which receives an anonymous app user ID and the App Store transaction — never your name, contacts, or content.

Children

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Subscriptions & purchases

OnThree offers auto-renewing monthly and annual subscriptions. Payment is charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase. Your subscription renews automatically unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period, and your account is charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your App Store account settings; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. A one-time lifetime purchase is also available — it is a non-consumable, one-time payment and does not auto-renew. Prices are shown in the app and may vary by region.
