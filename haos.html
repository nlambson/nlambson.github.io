Elite Career

A private, on-device career operating system.

Privacy Terms of Use Support

Privacy Policy

Elite Career is local-first. Your notes, documents, and self-model are stored on your device, not on our servers.

  • Camera: used only when you scan a document; capture is processed on-device with Apple VisionKit.
  • Microphone & speech recognition: used only when you dictate; transcription uses Apple's Speech framework.
  • Your vault: context, documents, and generated briefs are stored locally in the app's Application Support container.

Purchases

We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data. There is no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. Purchases are processed by Apple; subscription status is managed via RevenueCat, which receives an anonymous app user ID and the App Store transaction — never your name, contacts, or content.

Children

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Subscriptions & purchases

Elite Career offers auto-renewing monthly and annual subscriptions. Payment is charged to your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase. Your subscription renews automatically unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period, and your account is charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in your App Store account settings; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. Prices are shown in the app and may vary by region.
