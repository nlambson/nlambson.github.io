CounterSpy

Lightweight hidden-camera and tracker detector that runs entirely on your device.

Privacy Terms of Use Support

Privacy Policy

CounterSpy performs all detection on your device. It does not transmit scan results anywhere.

  • Camera: used only for the infrared lens-finder to spot hidden camera glints.
  • Bluetooth & local network: used to scan for nearby covert wireless devices. Results stay on your device.
  • Motion / magnetometer: used to detect the magnetic signature of hidden electronics.
  • Scan history: stored locally on your device.

Purchases
