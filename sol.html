Sol

Sunscreen timing and UV planning for your skin, your city, and the people you protect.

Privacy

Sol runs on your device. UV planning is computed locally from your inputs and Apple WeatherKit data.

  • Location: used only while you use the app, to fetch local UV/weather for your chosen city. Coordinates are used to look up weather and are not stored on our servers.
  • Weather: retrieved from Apple WeatherKit. Apple's handling is governed by Apple's privacy policy.
  • Your plan & profiles: skin type, SPF, and the people you track are stored on your device (and synced across your own Apple devices via Apple frameworks where applicable).
  • Notifications: reapply reminders are scheduled locally on your device.

Data collection

We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data. There is no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. Purchases are processed by Apple; subscription status is managed via RevenueCat (which receives an anonymous app user ID and the App Store transaction, never your name, contacts, or content).

Children

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.
